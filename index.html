Erin's Projects

LA Food Policy Council

Food System Dashboard

"Crowd Favorite" Award-winning project

LA Food Policy Council (LAFPC) "serves as [the] backbone organization for a network of over 400 organizations and agencies working for healthy, sustainable and fair food." In order to provide relevant and reliable data to these organizations, to policy-makers, and to members of the community, LAFPC needs a dashboard that displays this data in a digestible way that can be used by these organizations and individuals to effect change in their communities and to work toward LAFPC's Mission, "to ensure food is healthy, affordable, fair and sustainable for all."
General Assembly partnered with LA Food Policy Council for a Social Impact Hackathon where four teams of UX Designers and Developers partnered up to create a Food System Dashboard for LAFPC to better serve their community in the name of "Good Food For All."
